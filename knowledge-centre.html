Who Needs this Knowledge

The reason is simple

While every industry has its uniqueness, at the end of the day protecting data doesn't change. Similarly, protecting an ICS device in the energy sector is similar to protecting an MRI system in the medical industry. The most common businesses that will require this knowledge are those who have an extra need to protect their data or their customer data. With the rise of ransomware and increasingly sophisticated attacks, cybersecurity has become a topic of discussion for every business and board room. The reason is simple.

Poor cybersecurity threatens business continuity at its very core. Ignoring the problem entirely will only make the lack of cybersecurity more visible.

Government
While government was originally thought only to be nation-state level hacking, ransomware changed that. Whether local, state, or federal, criminals are attacking government targets at every level.
Financial
Criminals targeted the financial sector early on because quite literally, that is where the money is. The financial sector will remain a hot target and that sector needs to stay ahead of the ever-changing cybersecurity landscape.
Legal
Law offices often have highly sensitive information ranging from the names of protected witnesses to evidence photos to sensitive medical information. We can help protect their data and their clients.
System integrators, service vendors
A supply chain attack is a cyber-attack that seeks to damage an organization by targeting less-secure elements in their supply chain, namely their system integrators and vendors. Currently organizations are becoming more interested in confirming that their vendors are in full compliance with their Information security needs which oblige the vendors to strengthen their security posture to avoid any impacts on their clients and themselves due to its weaknesses
Public Utilities
What sector arguably has the biggest impact on society? An attack on public utilities could be devastating if we lose the very services we rely on daily. Without a doubt, the energy sector and other utilities are a target.
Medical
Healthcare has arguably felt the need for data sharing and data connectivity far more and far longer than any other industry. HIPAA and PHI (protected health information) are only the beginning.
Education
The education sector largely went untouched in cyberattacks. The rise of ransomware, the recognition of cyber insurance, and the realization that valuable data exists within the education system have changed that.
WFH
As many businesses starts to adopt working from home (WFH) model, many complications and cyber risks arised due to the limitations of the regular in-premise security solutions in providing the necessary security to roaming employees. We offer multiple solutions and services to extend our client’s cybersecurity coverage to their roaming employees
Everyone
Every industry has something to protect whether it is pricing, contracts, a customer list, or a "secret sauce" process. In addition, every business has a network, systems, and data to protect that it needs in order to run efficiently
